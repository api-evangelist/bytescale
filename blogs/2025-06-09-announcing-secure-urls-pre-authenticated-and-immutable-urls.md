Today we're excited to announce a new feature set for all Bytescale users: Secure URLs . Secure URLs are a new way to access private files in your Bytescale account. Instead of using Authorization headers, you can now generate pre-authenticated URLs. This makes it easier to share private files in places where setting custom headers isn’t practical — such as image tags, emails, and chat apps....
